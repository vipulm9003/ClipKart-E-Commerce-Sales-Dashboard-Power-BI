# 📊 ClipKart E-Commerce Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

The **ClipKart E-Commerce Sales Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence solution built using **Power BI**. It analyzes sales transactions across products, categories, and cities to provide meaningful business insights through dynamic visualizations and KPIs.

The dashboard enables users to monitor revenue performance, identify top-selling products, compare category-wise sales, and evaluate city-level performance, helping stakeholders make informed business decisions.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Power BI Desktop**
- **Power Query** (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**
- **CSV Dataset**
- **Data Modeling**
- **Data Visualization**

---

## 📂 Data Source

- **Source:** ClipKart E-Commerce Sales Dataset (CSV)
- **Data Type:** Sample/Fictional Retail Sales Data
- **Contains:**
  - Order ID
  - Product
  - Category
  - City
  - Quantity
  - Price
  - Revenue
  - Order Date

---

# ✨ Highlights

## 📌 Business Problem

E-commerce businesses generate thousands of sales transactions every day, making it difficult to manually monitor revenue trends, identify high-performing products, and compare sales performance across different categories and cities.

Without a centralized dashboard, decision-makers may struggle to quickly identify business opportunities and areas requiring improvement.

---

## 🎯 Dashboard Goal

The objective of this dashboard is to provide a single interactive view of overall sales performance by:

- Monitoring key business KPIs
- Tracking revenue across product categories
- Identifying top-performing products
- Comparing sales across different cities
- Understanding revenue distribution
- Supporting data-driven business decisions

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- ✅ Total Revenue KPI
- ✅ Total Orders KPI
- ✅ Total Quantity Sold KPI
- ✅ Average Order Value (AOV)
- ✅ Top Product Revenue
- ✅ Revenue by Category
- ✅ Top Revenue Products
- ✅ Top Performing Cities
- ✅ Category-wise Revenue Distribution
- ✅ Price vs Revenue Analysis
- ✅ Interactive Power BI Visualizations

---

## 💡 Key Insights
